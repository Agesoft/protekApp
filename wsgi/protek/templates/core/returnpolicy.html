SHIPPING & RETURN

Shipping Companies

Our industry use a wide variety of shipping companies so that our customers can get there items delivered on or before due dates. Shipping companies include USPS, DHL, UPS, FedEX, and Amazon shipping service; which is our main shipping system used. Customers can choose their shipping company of choice. Please note that free standard shipping isavailable on selected items. The delivery date for standard free shipping will take more than 10 business days. If shandard free shipping is selected, please await the arrival of the item which may result in a long wait time.

Shipping

Wordwide shipping is available on selected items. We ship to virtually any address in the world. However, there are restrictions on some products, and some products cannot be shipped to international destinations. Please Contact Us if there is an issue with your shipping address.

Whenever an order is placed, an estimated shipping and delivery date will be listed before checkout based on the shipping method choosen. Depending on the shipping provider choosen, shipping dates may vary. A longer delivery date for outbound location and shorter delivery dates for inbound location. Items might be delivered on or before estimated dates base on location.

Please note that the shipping rates for many items we sell are weight-based. The weight of any such item can be found on its detail page. To reflect the policies of the shipping companies we use, all weights will be rounded up to the next full pound.

Returns Policy

Customers may return new, unopened items within 14 days of delivery date for a full refund. ProteK Inc. will pay the return shipping costs if the return is a result of our error (ie. If an incorrect an or defective item is received etc.

Customers should expect to receive their refund within 21 days after returning their package to the return shipper. However, in many cases you will receive a refund more quickly. This time period includes the transit time for us to receive your return from the shipper (5 to 10 business days) and the time it takes us to process your return once we receive it (3 to 5 business days).

If customers need to return an item, please Contact Us with your order number and details about the product you would like to return. We will respond quickly with instructions for how to return items from your order.
